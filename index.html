About us

FIX

Pro Team Home Services is your trusted local partner for home maintenance and improvements in North Port, Florida, Sarasota County

We specialize in high-quality painting, flooring, tiling, and drywall repairs — the home projects that don’t require special permits, making it easy and convenient for homeowners to get reliable help.

We are insured and licensed, ensuring that every job we do is safe, professional, and up to the highest standards. No matter who you are — American, Ukrainian, Jewish, or from any background — we are here to help you maintain, refresh, and beautify your home.

 

At FixPro Team, we take care of your home problems and make improvements that last. From fixing damaged walls to installing new floors or freshening up a room with a new coat of paint, we handle every project with attention to detail and pride in our work. 

 

Our goal is simple: make your home better, more comfortable, and more beautiful. Contact us today to schedule an appointment or submit a request — we’re ready to bring your home improvement ideas to life.

Work Stages

At FixPro Team Home Services, we follow a clear and professional process to ensure every project is completed efficiently and to the highest standard. Here are the main phases of our work:

1. Consultation & Assessment

We start by discussing your needs, inspecting your home, and understanding the scope of the project. This ensures we provide the best solutions tailored to your home.

2. Planning & Scheduling

After the assessment, we create a detailed plan and schedule for your project, including timelines, materials, and costs.

3. Preparation

Before starting the main work, we prepare the area carefully, protecting your home and belongings to ensure a clean and safe environment.

4. Execution

Our skilled team carries out the work professionally, whether it’s painting, flooring, tiling, or drywall repairs. We focus on quality and attention to detail at every step

5. Final Inspection & Clean-Up

Once the work is completed, we inspect everything to ensure it meets our high standards. We also clean the area, leaving your home neat and ready to enjoy.

6. Customer Feedback & Follow-Up

Your satisfaction is our priority. We review the project with you, address any questions, and make sure you are happy with the results.
